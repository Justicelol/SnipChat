SnipChat — Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

SnipChat does not collect, store, track, sell, or share any personal information. We do not maintain databases, accounts, or identifiable records of any user.

Local Storage & Extension Configuration

The Extension may store non-personal settings such as crop rectangles and display preferences using Twitch’s Extension Configuration system or your browser’s local storage. These values remain on your device or within Twitch’s systems.

No Third-Party Analytics

SnipChat contains no tracking scripts, analytics services, cookies, or advertising networks.

Pro Subscriptions

If you choose to upgrade to SnipChat Pro, payment may be processed by Stripe. SnipChat does not handle or store payment information. You should review Stripe’s Privacy Policy for details.

Children’s Privacy

This Extension is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data.
